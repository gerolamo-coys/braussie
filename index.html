About Braussie

We're a passionate small business bringing the authentic taste of Brazilian brigadeiros to Brisbane, Australia. Each brigadeiro is lovingly handcrafted using traditional techniques and premium ingredients, creating the perfect fusion of Brazilian heritage and Australian quality.

Our name "Braussie" represents the beautiful blend of Brazilian ("Br") culture with Australian ("Aussie") warmth and hospitality.
